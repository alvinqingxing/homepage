Alvin Cheng-Hin Lim

I am a writer at HTX (Home Team Science & Technology Agency) and before this was a web developer at Singapore Discovery Centre. I learned to code at Le Wagon with support from the Infocomm Media Development Authority of Singapore's Tech Immersion and Placement Programme (TIPP). I occasionally write apps -- for example the no-code meditation app Serenity Now -- and am a Google Cloud-certified Associate Cloud Engineer.

Before my career transition to technology, I was in academia. I received my M.A. in Philosophy from the National University of Singapore and my Ph.D. in Political Science from the University of Hawaii at Manoa, and have taught at Pannasastra University of Cambodia, the University of Hawaii at Manoa, and the American University of Nigeria.

Publications

My publications include Cambodia and the Politics of Aesthetics and China and Southeast Asia in the Xi Jinping Era. My books may be available at a library near you. My full writing portfolio can be found at Academia.edu.
