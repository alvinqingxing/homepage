Alvin Lim

Educator and Writer turned Coder

Background

Alvin is learning to code at Codecademy and Le Wagon.

He received his Ph.D. in Political Science from the University of Hawaii at Manoa in 2011.

During his career as an international educator, he taught in Cambodia (Pannasastra University of Cambodia), the United States (the University of Hawaii at Manoa), and Nigeria (the American University of Nigeria).
